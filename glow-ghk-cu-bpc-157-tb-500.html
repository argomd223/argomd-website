Safety & Compliance

Glow is a compounded prescription medication prepared by a state-licensed 503A compounding pharmacy under Section 503A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act. Compounded drugs are not FDA-approved and the FDA does not verify the safety, effectiveness, or labeling of compounded medications.

GHK-Cu, BPC-157, and TB-500 are prescribed as a compounded preparation based on the prescribing physician's clinical judgment. None are FDA-approved for a specific indication. In 2023, FDA placed BPC-157 and TB-500 on the 503A Category 2 list — substances that may present significant safety risks. The regulatory status is active; verify before starting.

Prescription required. A consultation with a U.S.-licensed physician is necessary to determine if this product is appropriate for you. Individual results vary.
