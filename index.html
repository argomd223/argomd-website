Frequently Asked Questions

What states do you serve?

All 50 states are served by ArgoMD. We work with licensed physicians and pharmacies nationwide to ensure you have access to treatment regardless of your location.

Who are your pharmacies?

ArgoMD partners with Rush Pharmacy, a state-regulated pharmacy that specializes in compounded medications. All medications are prepared in licensed facilities that meet or exceed regulatory standards.

Who is your provider network?

ArgoMD works with Locum Tele, a network of board-certified physicians licensed to practice telemedicine across all 50 states. Every doctor in our network has specific training in weight management and obesity medicine.

How long does shipping take?

Once your prescription is approved, medications are typically shipped within 24-48 hours and delivered within 3-5 business days. All shipments include tracking and are packaged discreetly.

How do consultations work?

Consultations are conducted entirely online through our secure platform. You'll complete a medical questionnaire, and a licensed physician will review your information. If approved, your prescription is sent directly to our pharmacy partner. No video calls or phone appointments required.

What is your refund policy?

If you are not approved for treatment after your consultation, you will receive a full refund. Once medication has been shipped, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact support@argomd.com for assistance with any order issues.
