## Copy Strategy Notes

**Evolve Principles Applied:**
- **Pattern Interrupt:** Every ad opens with a contrast or reframe ("Most... Retatrutide...")
- **Unique Mechanism:** "Triple-receptor agonist (GLP-1 + GIP + Glucagon)" is front and center
- **Credibility Stack:** Licensed U.S. compounding pharmacy + physician-prescribed + telehealth
- **Risk Reversal:** Physician oversight, personalized care, discreet delivery
- **Scarcity:** "Limited availability," "introductory pricing," "spots fill"
- **Clear CTA:** Consistent 2-minute quiz framing with 👇 directional cue
- **Compliance:** All claims use "support" language; no cure/treatment promises

**Competitor Differentiation:**
- Competitor: "Retatrutide now available. Produced by a licensed U.S. compounding pharmacy..."
- Our approach: Lead with mechanism + benefit first, then credibility, then offer. Emotionally resonant hooks matched to creative aesthetic.
